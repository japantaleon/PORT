Behr.

My second year back at Behr was very Research Heavy! I got to understand different demographic needs and wants. My research was used to create new direction to Boost User Engagement within Behr Designer Stories. Designer Stories are found under our Inspiration section on Behr.com, there they serve to give consumers inspiration for their home related DIY Projects. The page holds numerous DIY blogs/tutorials created by influencers, with each having their own creative touch.

More Human

I wanted to understand each demographic at a Emotional Level to get a better sense of who they are. Designing a home is very sentimental task, which is why I felt emotional understanding was important to create engaging content.
